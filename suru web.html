Computer Science Engineering (CSE) is an academic programme that integrates the field of Computer Engineering and Computer Science. It is one of the most sought-after courses among engineering students. The course contains a plethora of topics but emphasises the basics of computer programming and networking.

ECE

Electronics and Communication Engineering” is one of the fastest growing field of engineering courses. It involves the transmission of information across the channel. The course concept is to focus mainly on the communication part with the knowledge of the electronics field.
